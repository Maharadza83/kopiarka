Welcome to Kopiarka!

Welcome to our e-learning platform, designed to facilitate students in sharing study notes to help each other in learning.

You can log in, register, add notes, and browse through the list of notes from other students.

Our goal is to create a friendly environment where students can support each other in learning and develop their skills.

Why join our community?

  • You will find helpful peers with similar interests and educational challenges.
  • You can share your notes and use notes from other students.
  • We offer the opportunity to develop interpersonal skills through participation in discussions and sharing ideas.
  • Our platform is user-friendly and ensures the security of your data.
